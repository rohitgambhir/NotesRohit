Principles of Management

  • Unit-II:Planning and decision making - concept, purpose and process of planning, types of plans, strategic planning, tactical planning and operational planning, goal setting, MBO, decision making: nature and process, behavioral aspects of decision making, forms of group decision making in organizations

  • Unit-IV:Motivation: concept and nature, need hierarchy and motivation-hygiene theories of motivation, Management control:- nature, purpose and process of controlling, kinds of control system, prerequisites of effective control system, controlling techniques.
